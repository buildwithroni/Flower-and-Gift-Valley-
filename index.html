About Us

Founded by Sovan Adak in 2016, Ixora Flower is a premium gifting & floral delivery brand. We don’t have a walk-in store, but our Bidhannagar dispatch center coordinates same-day deliveries with 6 in-house delivery boys and 30+ partners across West Bengal.
